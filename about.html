Meet the Owner

Christopher Jackson

Founder & Licensed Arborist

I'm a born-and-raised Connecticut native who found my calling working with trees. With almost a decade of hands-on experience in every aspect of tree care — from the ground up to 80-foot canopies — I started MacKenzie Tree LLC because I believed clients deserved better service than what was available.

The name? That's easy. MacKenzie is my daughter — MacKenzie Elizabeth — and building something worth her name has always been my motivator. Every job I take, I treat with the same care I'd bring to my own property.

As a member of the Connecticut Tree Protection Association, I stay current on the latest techniques, safety standards, and tree health practices. Because trees aren't just obstacles — they're a vital part of our environment, and I take that seriously.
