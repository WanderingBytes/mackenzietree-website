What We Do

Comprehensive Tree Care Services

From a single branch to a full property transformation — we bring expert craftsmanship to every job.

Comprehensive Tree Care

Planting, fertilizing, pruning, cabling, and insect/disease treatment. We diagnose tree health issues and consult on landscape projects to keep your trees thriving.

Tree Removal

Safe, efficient removal whether it's a hazard, too close to structures, blocking sunlight for your pool or solar panels. We handle all sizes with precision and care.

Landscape Design & Installation

Planning a pool, deck, or patio? Need to level your property? We provide ideas, plantings, and pricing for full landscaping projects of any scale.

Ornamental Trimming & Hedges

We specialize in pruning and trimming smaller ornamental trees, hedges, and bushes for both health and aesthetic appeal. Maintenance programs available.

Stump Grinding & Removal

Complete stump grinding or full removal — perfect for driveway expansions, new patios, or just reclaiming that lost square footage in your lawn.

Wood Chips & Firewood Delivery

Offering 12–20 yard dumpster loads of hardwood logs at discounted rates, pre-cut for splitting. Wood chips available for property edging and ground cover.

About MacKenzie Tree

Built on Safety, Reliability & a Love of the Land

Hi there — I'm Christopher Jackson, a Connecticut native and the proud founder of MacKenzie Tree, LLC. This company was born from a genuine passion for tree care and named after my daughter, MacKenzie Elizabeth.

With nearly a decade of hands-on experience, I've built this business on one simple promise: deliver the safest, most reliable tree care available. We don't cut corners — and we definitely don't cut the wrong branches.

As a member of the Connecticut Tree Protection Association, we're committed to the preservation of your property and the health of Connecticut's trees.
